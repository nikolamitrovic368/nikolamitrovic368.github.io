About

We are family!

We’re Nikola and Tanja Mitrovic — a husband-and-wife team dedicated to building beautiful and high-performing Shopify experiences. As a family, we work together on every project. Tanja focuses on UI/UX design, crafting intuitive interfaces and visually engaging storefronts that reflect each brand’s identity. Nikola handles the technical side — developing Shopify themes, customizing storefronts, and integrating APIs, including Shopify Storefront API, third-party services, and custom app solutions. Together, we combine design and development to deliver end-to-end Shopify solutions that are fast, scalable, and conversion-focused. With a strong sense of collaboration and passion for what we do, we’re proud to help brands grow — one project at a time.
